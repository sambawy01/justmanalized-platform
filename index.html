The Brand

Hand-finished. Sun-ready. One of one.

Every Just Manalized hat starts as hand-woven straw and is finished by hand — turquoise conchos, crystal rhinestones, jewelled and beaded bands. Curated and styled at Abu Tig Marina, El Gouna, for the days that run from the beach into the sunset.

The Story

Born by the Red Sea.

Just Manalized began at the water’s edge in El Gouna — a love of the western hat reimagined for the Red Sea. We take classic woven straw and hand-finish each piece with conchos, stones and beadwork, so the hat you wear to brunch at Abu Tig is just as ready for sunset on the marina.

The Details

  1. Hand-woven straw breathable, lightweight, made for the heat
  2. Hand-set hardware turquoise & silver conchos, crystal rhinestones, jewelled bands
  3. Adjustable fit one size with an inner drawstring
