# Just Manalized — Brand Foundation (v1)

*Made in El Gouna. Going International. Handmade, one-of-a-kind coastal-cowgirl
hats born on the Red Sea, worn everywhere.* (Source: brand-foundation.pdf)

## 1. The name is the story
**Just Manalized = Manal + manual (by hand).** The founder's name (**Manal**)
and the method are fused into one word. Don't explain it apologetically — make
it the origin myth. Every hat is *manualized*: touched, shaped and finished by
Manal's hands, never machine, never duplicated. Handle / domain: **justmanalized**.

## 2. Positioning
The handmade Western-boho hat brand from El Gouna, where every piece is
one-of-one and hand-embellished by its maker. For women who want a statement
piece, not a souvenir — the resort & marina set, festival-goers, and the global
coastal-cowgirl audience. The moat: the trend is everywhere; the handmade,
named-origin, one-of-one version is not.

## 3. Brand pillars
1. **One of one** — no two hats alike; scarcity is the luxury mechanic.
2. **Made in El Gouna** — Red Sea provenance as a mark of origin ("Made in ___").
3. **Coastal cowgirl** — sun, marina, a little untamed. Freedom you can wear.
4. **Collectible, not disposable** — pieces you keep and name.

## 4. Visual codes ("a Manal")
Lock and repeat: **hero trio** = turquoise cabochon + cowrie shells +
antique-silver concho · cream macramé fringe and tassels · wine/burgundy band
against natural straw · contrast hand-stitching (the visible human touch) ·
occasional beaded creature motifs (scorpion, bull-skull) — reserve for a
signature series.

## 5. Color palette
| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Base / paper | Sand | `#F4ECDC` |
| Primary dark / text | Espresso | `#2B1D14` |
| Primary accent | Wine | `#6E2B33` |
| Pop accent | Turquoise | `#2F9C95` |
| Neutral | Cream | `#F1E7D3` |
| Metallic detail | Brass | `#B08D57` |

Wine + turquoise on sand and espresso — distinctive, pulled from the product.

## 6. Typography
- **Display / headlines:** high-contrast editorial serif — **Fraunces** (free now;
  upgrade to Ogg or Canela later).
- **Body / UI:** humanist sans — **Inter** (or Geist).
- **Signature accent:** a refined script, very sparing, for Manal's hand-touches
  (e.g. each hat's name). Avoid literal Western/saloon fonts.

## 7. Logo direction
The brand owns a logo device: **the concho medallion** — a stylized circular
concho-with-turquoise emblem that works as favicon, social avatar, leather
hang-tag, and the stamp inside the hat band. Wordmark: **JUST MANALIZED** in the
display serif, wide letter-spacing. Signature: a script *Manal*.

## 8. Voice & tone
Confident, warm, a little wild. Short sentences. Sun on them. Never corporate.
- "Made once. Never again."
- "Born at the marina. Packed for anywhere."
- "This one's the only one."
- "Shop our premium collection of artisanal headwear."

## 9. Content system
- **AI imagery** → hero banners, lookbook, ads, social mood, the marina fantasy.
- **Real photography** → every product page (each hat is one-of-one; the
  customer must see the actual piece).
